## Brand Guidelines

Follow these guidelines to maintain consistency across your documentation.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Colors" icon="palette">
    Primary brand color: `{ #3B82F6 }`. Use it for headings, buttons, and accents.
    
    Secondary palette:
    | Color       | Hex Code     | Usage                  |
    |-------------|--------------|------------------------|
    | Primary     | `{ #3B82F6 }` | Main actions, links   |
    | Success     | `{ #10B981 }` | Confirmations         |
    | Danger      | `{ #EF4444 }` | Errors, deletions     |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Typography" icon="type">
    Use clear, readable fonts. Headings in bold, body text at 16px minimum.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Icons" icon="zap">
    Leverage Lucide icons. Match style: line weight 2, consistent sizing.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Apply `{ #3B82F6 }` for all interactive elements to reinforce your brand.
</Callout>
